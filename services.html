Meet the Founder

Melissa Jane

Founder & Environmental Health Cleaning Consultant

This business didn't begin in wellness — it began in survival. Melissa was diagnosed with Dysautonomia and Mast Cell Activation Syndrome (MCAS), spending years navigating chronic inflammation and systemic chaos as a professional patient.

It wasn't until she radically transformed the environments she lived in that her nervous system began to regulate and true healing could finally begin. Her journey revealed a truth most people overlook: the body doesn't heal in isolation — it heals in relationship to its environment.

Now she helps families design healthier home ecosystems rooted in nervous-system safety and environmental awareness.
